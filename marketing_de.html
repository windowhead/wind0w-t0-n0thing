Various Projects

Berufsmeisterschaften 2025

As a part of a challange, students were allowed to take part in the Berufsmesiterschaften. You get around 3 hours of time to complete an assignment.
In this case it was a small marketing capaign for a company called Vintage finds. The logo was given while you were tasked with designing a promotional post, a visual and a marketing strategy, all in english.

Due to the short time given its a big challange, but overall very fun to do.

Mini capaign: Fruibi Soda

In our marketing class we made quite a few projects, this was one of the last ones we were able to work on. This was a solo project and had to be done within 6 lessons (each 45 mins.)
Created were a logo, a marketing strategy and a poster.

Homepages

In my second year of my apprenticeship we were learning the basics of animation in After Effects or similar programs.
Since I already knew some basics, I challanged myself to 40 seconds of completely hand-drawn animation in the illustration program Krita.
Through this first touchpoint with handdrawn animation I can now create complex animations, all with the power of my imagination.

Video Project: Aftermovie Mustang & Shelby Meeting Zug

As a helper of the Mustang Club of Switzerland I was invited to the annual Mustang & Shelby Meeting.
During my breaks I had from the Merchandise stand, I was out flying my drone and shooting pictures for the clubs homepage that I also partly manage.

Video Project: Brainsparks

As my friend entered the 4th year of their apprenticeship, they also stoof before the challange of a short film. I offered to help produce it and help them out behind the scenes.
In this film I helped with scripting, small camera work, setdressing, behind the scenes, audio on set and postproduction of the behind the scenes video. I was also one of the actors in the film, with a smaller role as the only other character.
